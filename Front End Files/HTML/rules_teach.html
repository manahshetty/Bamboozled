Instructions


  • An English teacher can upload 'Vocab Lists' much like the 'Weekly Vocab Builder' concepts in schools today.

  • On the top navigation bar, select 'Upload Vocab List'->'Your Grade/ Standard' to be directed to the Upload page.

  • Attach the list of words as a JPEG or PNG file.

  • A customised gameboard will now be generated with the deck consisting of synonyms for the attached words.
