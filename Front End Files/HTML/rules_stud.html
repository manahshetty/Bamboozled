Rules :


On opening the quiz page, a question, and a deck of 25 cards will be visible. Students need to choose the five MOST suitable options related to the given clue.

Students will select the subject and the chapter they are taking the quiz for, and there will be five questions for each chapter.

A correct guess leads to a +4 in the score, while a wrong one incurs a -2.
