About Us


Here's some tid-bits about who we are and what we do! :)

We are a bunch of sophomores dedicated to channeling technology to empower the world today.
EduForTreasure brings to you an Innovative and Fun approach to online education for the children of today.
The lockdown finds education put to a standstill with massive drops in student engagement,
due to virtual classrooms. In this day, where education of the youth stands to make our future,
it becomes vital to incentivize this learning process.

It is in light of this, that we bring to you EduForTreasure,
where learning is fun and interactive.
